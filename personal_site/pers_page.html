Publications, during the collaboration between HFRI & DANA Lab, UTH

Katsaros Dimitrios, Assoc.Prof. @ UTH Fragkou Evangelia, PhD Student @ UTH

Information

The aim of this page is to present the progress of the 2-year (2022-2024) granted project, entitled: "Deep Learning and its Applications in Big Data Networks Performance Improvement", under the 3rd Call of PhD Fellowships (Fellowship Number: 5631), supported by the Hellenic Foundation for Research and Innovation (HFRI). Fragkou Evangelia, as a member of the DANA Lab and under the supervision of Associate Professor, Dr.Dimitrio Katsaro has earned this grant, in order to carry our her PhD studies. Specifically, in this page we present the articles, being accepted in both Journals and Conferences and they are also found in various bibliographic databases: DBLP, ACM, Google Scholar, Scopus.
